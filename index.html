Michael Homolka

Michael Homolka’s debut poetry collection, Antiquity, was published by Sarabande Books. His poems have appeared in magazines such as The New Yorker, The Threepenny Review, Ploughshares, Prairie Schooner, Boulevard, Antioch Review, Agni, Denver Quarterly, Colorado Review, Poetry Daily, and Verse Daily. A graduate of Bennington College’s MFA Program, he currently teaches high school English at Sponsors for Educational Opportunity.

“The poems in Antiquity very much abandon themselves to language, to the collective poetic endeavor, and they do so in a rich, textured, and sustained voice …”

—Mary Ruefle

“Homolka’s wry intelligence and off-kilter wit permeate these poems, along with a deep, unfashionable irony …”

—Harvard Review
